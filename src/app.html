Your Questions,
Answered

"What if nobody downloads the app?"

We share that risk. That's why we validate ideas before building and focus on apps that solve real problems for real people.

"How much equity do I give up?"

Depends on what you bring. Someone with a validated idea and an audience keeps more equity. Someone joining our researched opportunity has a different split. We're transparent.

"Do I need technical skills?"

No. We handle all the technical work. You need to understand your users and the problem you're solving, but we build everything.

"What about app store approvals?"

We handle that. Apple and Google have guidelines we follow. Web apps don't need approval at all.

"How do we make money from the app?"

Subscriptions, one-time purchases, freemium models, in-app purchases, ads, or lead generation. We choose the model that fits your app and users.

"What if I don't have an audience?"

Not required. We can drive cold traffic through ads, ASO (App Store Optimization), content marketing, and partnerships.

"How long until the app makes money?"

In some cases, apps can take time to get traction, but we optimize aggressively to get profits ASAP.

"What about updates and maintenance?"

We handle ongoing updates, bug fixes, and feature additions. Apps aren't "launch and forget" - they need continuous improvement.

Your Questions, Answered

“What if I’m not ‘expert’ enough?”

You don’t need to be the world’s #1 expert. You just need to know more than your target customer and be able to help them get results. We help you position your expertise effectively.

“What if nobody buys?”

We share that risk. That’s why we validate ideas, create compelling offers, and drive qualified traffic. We don’t launch products we don’t believe will sell.

“How much equity do I give up?”

Depends on what you bring. Someone with expertise, an audience, and content creation ability keeps more. Someone joining our researched opportunity has a different split. Always transparent.

“Do I need an audience first?”

Helps, but not required. We can drive cold traffic through ads, partnerships, content marketing, and SEO. An audience accelerates success but isn’t mandatory.

“What if I hate being on camera?”

Not all digital products need video. Ebooks, templates, and written content work great. If it’s a course, we can use screen recordings, slides, or audio. We adapt to your strengths.

“How do we price the product?”

We research your market, analyze competitors, and test pricing. We start with what makes sense and adjust based on real data.

“What about customer support?”

We set up systems that ensure smooth and seamless customer support, so every inquiry is managed efficiently without you having to handle each one personally.

“How long until I see revenue?”

Digital products can generate revenue quickly once launched. We optimize quickly so that profits can start coming in from month one.
